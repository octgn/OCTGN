Icons needed for electron-builder packaging:

Windows:
  icon.ico - Already present (copied from main OCTGN project)

macOS:
  icon.icns - NEEDED: Convert icon.ico to .icns format (1024x1024)
  Tip: Use iconutil on macOS or an online converter

Linux:
  icon.png - NEEDED: 512x512 PNG version of the app icon
  Alternatively, provide a set of PNGs at standard sizes (16x16, 32x32,
  48x48, 64x64, 128x128, 256x256, 512x512) in this directory.
  electron-builder will pick them up automatically from the icons/ folder.
