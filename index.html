What is Octgn?

Octgn is a platform for creating, sharing, and playing card games and (if created)board games.

Who should use Octgn?

Anyone and everyone who likes playing games online with their friends!

Why should I use Octgn?

It's flexable, user friendly, and free.

Where can I get these, so called, games?

Just do a search on google for Octgn Games. There are many game makers out there dedicated to making quality games for the Octgn platform.
